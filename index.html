Quick reference

10 things to remember

1

Activate the IAM ID before school starts

Arrives by post in August. Activate at iam.education.lu — needed for WebUntis, Teams, and every school platform.

2

Top up the myCard before day one

Credit at restopolis.lu — your child's payment card for the school restaurant and cafeteria.

3

Report absences on day 1

Contact the Student Secretariat on the first day. Return: hand a written excuse to the class teacher within 3 days. Full process →

4

Medical certificate from the 3rd sick day

Required from the 3rd consecutive day of illness. A parent excuse alone is not enough after day 2.

5

Check WebUntis every day

Timetable changes, homework, test dates, and absence records all live here. Install the app. Setup guide ↗

6

Locker deposit: €20 in cash

New students pay €20 cash to the class teacher. Refundable when your child permanently leaves LLIS.

7

Photocopies fee — invoiced in October

Annual contribution per student. Invoice arrives by email in early October. FP/VP students exempt.

8

Financial support deadline: 31 October

Applications must reach SePAS by 31 October. Handled in complete confidence. Late applications cannot be accepted.

9

School books are free — order at mybooks.lu

Log in at mybooks.lu with the IAM ID.

10

Contact teachers by email — not via Teams

Format: firstname.lastname@education.lu. Teams messages to teachers may not be seen. Contacts →
